Mirage Casino

Provably fair. Every round.

Every wager and payout runs through one audited ledger — no hidden balances, no rigged reels. Crash publishes a cryptographic commitment before each round starts and reveals the seed after it crashes, so anyone can recompute the result themselves. Every other game rolls on Python's secrets CSPRNG, server-side, before you ever see an outcome.

1. Commit

Before betting closes, the server publishes sha256(server_seed) — a one-way hash that reveals nothing about the outcome, but locks it in.

2. Play

The round runs. The multiplier climbs until it crashes at a point already fixed by the hidden seed from step one.

3. Reveal & verify

The seed is published. Recompute the hash and the crash point yourself — every past round is checkable, forever.
